ABOUT


Ahoy ahoy, I'm Kirsten!

I am a creative developer and amateur designer who's passionate about building digital experiences that nurture community, inclusivity and knowledge. I am a graduate of Generation USA / Skillgrowth's Full Stack Jr. Java Developer full-time cohort.


Whether the internet or the outdoors, I love exploring, and I apply this curious mindset to my projects. I'm happiest when I am using my passions and interests to bring ideas to life and give the end result that 'add to favorites ASAP' type of energy.
